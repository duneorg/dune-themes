This demo site showcases a landing-archetype Dune theme — a designed home
page, inner pages for CTAs, and a small blog so post/list templates stay
exercised.

## What to try

- Start on the **home** hero / banner layout
- Open **Blog** for the post listing
- Visit **About**, **Search**, and **Archives** from the menu or homepage tiles
- Trigger a **404** with a made-up URL to check the themed error page
