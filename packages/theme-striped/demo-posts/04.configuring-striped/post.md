Striped keeps a small config surface — enough to brand the sidebar without
inventing schemes upstream never had.

| Option | Default | Notes |
|--------|---------|--------|
| `show_html5up_credit` | on | Gates the HTML5 UP link (CC BY). Keep on unless you hold a Pixelarity license. |
| `sidebar_tagline` | `A Dune site` | Line under the site title in the sidebar |
| `footer_text` | empty | Copyright name; falls back to the site title |

There is **no** dark mode or color-scheme preset in this port. Upstream
Striped is a single light design; a Dune dark lift may land later as an
optional enhancement, not as “fidelity.”
