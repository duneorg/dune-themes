Product sites often outgrow a brochure homepage: release notes, tutorials,
and changelogs want a real post listing. Gale keeps that in one package so
the accent colour, header CTA, and typography stay consistent from the
hero into the blog cards.

Use the homepage collection `limit` to teaser a few posts under the
features, and let `/blog/` paginate the rest. Archives and search cover
the “I know it exists somewhere” path.
