Herald is for sites that want the first viewport to feel like a magazine
cover — a dark masthead, a full-bleed hero, then a wide feed of excerpts
with optional cover thumbnails and author lines.

That mood comes from Ghost Casper without being a pixel-perfect port:
bold headlines, generous whitespace, and chrome that stays out of the way
so photography and titles can carry the page.

None of that requires Ghost. Herald implements the pattern as a
Dune-native theme with the usual templates, search, archives, and config
panel.
